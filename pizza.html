About Catering


pizza

In 2009, at the request of an Italian, Neapolitan pizza was registered in the European Union as a traditional dish and in 2017 pizza making was included in the UNESCO List of Intangible Cultural Heritage. Rafael Esposito is often credited with creating the modern form of pizza known today.Pizza is one of the most famous Italian dishes that everyone loves. It is a base of dough covered with a layer of tomato sauce, grated cheese, and other ingredients as desired, then baked in the oven. The origin of pizza goes back to Italy, and it has become one of the world's most famous and beloved dishes.
